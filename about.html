About

I grew up mostly in Costa Rica, where nature, culture, and community shaped my curiosity about people and creativity early on. I’m currently studying Fashion Business at IED Madrid, and I’ve always been drawn to spaces where art, fashion, and culture bring people together.

Because most of my work is freelance, I think of myself as a creative handyman for whatever an event or project needs. I love multidisciplinarity and I’m ready to solve problems big or small, whether that’s managing email lists, sewing a projector curtain, sourcing a sound system day‑of, or styling a runway.

I’m a people‑driven creative and I’m grateful for the connections I’ve made in Madrid in a short time. My wide circle of fellow creatives, who are also friends, makes hosting events feel especially joyful. I can help them grow, and they help me grow right back. That creative community fuels my work every day.
